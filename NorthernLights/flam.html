Flam


Flam is situated in the innermost part of the Aurlandsfjord, a tributary of the 204 kilometres long and up to 1,308 metres deep Sognefjord. Surrounded by steep mountains, thundering waterfalls and narrow valleys, this area is a paradise for those who seek unique and spectacular experiences in nature. Explore how to see and exerperience the majesty of Flam.

Flam Railroad
The Flam railway has been named one of the most beautiful train journeys in the world and is one of the leading tourist attractions in Norway. Flamsbana, known around the globe as one of the most spectacular train journeys in the world, was named the world's best train journey by Lonely Planet in 2014. A journey with this amazing railway from sea level in Flam by the fjord to the mountain tops has an altitude difference of almost 900 meters, with a gradient of 1m in 18. One of 20 tunnels on the Flam railway spirals inside the mountain and the journey offers views that will take your breath away.. repeatedly. Undredal
Undredal is a sleepy village nestled in a valley in Western Norway. It is famous for its brown goat cheese, or "gjetost," which is produced by eight farms using traditional methods. Undredal welcomes tourists and is accessible by a short taxi trip from the Flam rail station. It offers lodging, camping, a café, and of course a shop where you can purchase goat cheese. The village is truly breathtaking. It is good to know places like this still exist in the world. Stay the night and catch a boat to continue on your tour of the fiord at the dock in town...just turn on the flashing dock light to catch the boat. Nærøyfjord
Nærøyfjord in Sognefjord - Western Norway - is one of the worlds most beautiful and dramatic fjords! The name Nærøyfjord origin from the norse god 'Njord', the god of the seafarer and the sea On the fjord cruise, you travel passed the beautiful villages of Aurland and Undredal before the boat enters the Nærøyfjord, one of the narrowest fjords in Europe. This arm of the Sognefjord is perhaps the most dramatic fjord in Norway, and is on UNESCO's World Heritage List

Things to know

When travelling from Oslo to Bergen with Norway in a Nutshell or some comparable combination of train, boat and bus it is strongly reccomended that a porter service for your luggage is hired. In that manner, you will not have to transport your luggage from train to boat,to bus, to train. Just pack a small overnighht bag if you are taking the time to enjoy the fiord. Your luggage will be waiting at your hotel when you arrive in Bergen.

When you get off the train in Flam, after shopping in the gift shops and you need to get to your lodgings in Undredal or any of the other amazing towns along the fiord. There is only one taxi service Aurlund Taxi consisting of three taxis. Small but personable, very accomodating and very reasonably priced.
